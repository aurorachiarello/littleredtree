  • little red tree

    Little Red Tree is a home based design studio, specialising in designing and printing wedding invitations and stationery.

    With a background in Graphic Design and a love of designing and creating things, Little Red Tree has developed a range of stylish, modern and vibrant invitations and accessories for all special occasions. Our collection of contemporary wedding stationery can be personalised to suit your individual style, whether it be classic and traditional, elegant or modern. In addition to custom wedding stationery, Little Red Tree also specialises in Graphic design, Corporate Invitations, Baby Announcement Cards, Christening and Birthday Invitations, and also produces a range of handmade children’s homeware and accessories.
