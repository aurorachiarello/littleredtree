a little about us...

Little Red Tree is a small graphic design studio, specialising in designing and printing wedding invitations and stationery.

With a background in Graphic Art and Design and a love of designing and creating things, Little Red Tree has developed a range of stylish, modern and vibrant invitations and accessories for all special occasions.
In addition to custom wedding stationery, Little Red Tree also specialises in Graphic Design, Corporate Invitations, Baby Announcement Cards, Christening and Birthday Invitations and also produces a range of handmade children’s homeware and accessories

HANDMADE

As a result of my passion and love of decorative pattern and beautiful fabric, Little Red Tree has created a range of fun and unique accessories for children’s interiors.

Our exclusive collection are all individually handmade using a selection of modern, vintage and classic fabrics. Our aim is to create products that are very unique and distinctive and will be loved not only by the little ones but also appeal to the adults. They are perfect for that special little gift or for adding a personal touch to a child’s bedroom or playroom.

Alphabet Pillows are approximately 35cm high and are filled with a non allergenic filling. Width will vary depending on each letter.
Doorangers are approximately 14cm high and can be made using the fabric of your choice.
Frames come in white and are 35.5cm square in size. Frames for girls have 3 dimensional element added to the fabric, such as hand glitter and buttons.
Photo Albums are 23cm x 33cm in size and contain approximately 25 pages of black card and 25 pages of acid free tracing paper in between. The Albums can fit up to 100 photos. Additional pages can be added.
Photo Albums and Frames can be personalised with the child’s name.
Heart Garlands are 2 metres in length and include 10 doublesided hearts, hand stuffed with polyfil. Hearts are 9 x 12cm and joined together using white crochet cotton lace ribbon.
Flag Buntings are 3.5 metres in length and include 18 doublesided flags. Flags are 11 x 11cm and joined together using white satin ribbon.
